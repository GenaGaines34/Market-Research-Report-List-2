<p><p>Energy harvesting devices capture and convert ambient energy from various sources into usable power. The light energy harvesting market focuses on solar cells that convert sunlight into electricity. Vibration energy harvesting utilizes mechanical vibrations to generate power, often through piezoelectric materials. Thermal energy harvesting captures waste heat and converts it into electrical energy, leveraging thermoelectric generators. Electromagnetic or RF energy harvesting derives energy from radio waves and electromagnetic fields, enabling devices to operate without traditional power sources, enhancing sustainability and convenience.</p></p>
