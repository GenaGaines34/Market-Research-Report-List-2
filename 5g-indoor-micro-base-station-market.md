<p><ul><li>Distributed</li><li>Integrated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G Indoor Micro Base Station market can be categorized into two main types: distributed and integrated. Distributed micro base stations operate as separate units, connected through a central management system, allowing for flexible placement throughout a facility. In contrast, integrated micro base stations combine the radio unit and processing functionality into a single unit, simplifying installation and reducing space requirements. Both types aim to enhance indoor coverage, capacity, and user experience within varying environments such as offices and public venues.</p></p>
