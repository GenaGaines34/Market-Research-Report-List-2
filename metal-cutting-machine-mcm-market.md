<p><p>The Metal Cutting Machine (MCM) market encompasses various technologies used for precision cutting of metals. Laser cutting machines utilize high-powered lasers for intricate and clean cuts, ideal for complex shapes. Waterjet cutting machines employ high-pressure water mixed with abrasives for versatile material applications without heat distortion. Plasma cutting machines use ionized gas to cut electrically conductive materials quickly. Flame cutting machines, or oxy-fuel cutters, rely on a flame for thicker materials. Other methods may include EDM and mechanical cutting techniques, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
