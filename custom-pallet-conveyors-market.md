<p><p>Custom pallet conveyors play a vital role in material handling, featuring various types designed for specific applications. Drag chain conveyors use linked chains to transport heavy pallets, offering robust support. Roller conveyors, either driven or gravity, facilitate smooth movement, with driven rollers powered to enhance speed. Plastic type conveyors utilize lightweight, corrosion-resistant components for cleaner operations. Slat type conveyors consist of slats that provide a solid surface, ideal for moving irregularly shaped loads, ensuring versatility in warehouse and manufacturing environments.</p></p>
