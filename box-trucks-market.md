<p><ul><li>Single Bridge Box Trucks</li><li>Double Bridge Box Trucks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The box truck market is categorized into two main types: single bridge and double bridge box trucks. Single bridge box trucks feature a simple design with a single axle, making them ideal for urban deliveries and smaller loads, providing maneuverability and efficiency. In contrast, double bridge box trucks have a more complex structure with two axles, allowing for greater weight capacity and stability, making them suitable for transporting larger cargo over longer distances. Each type serves distinct logistical needs in the transportation industry.</p></p>
