<p><p>The Fiber Optic Receiver market encompasses various connector types, each designed for specific applications. FC (Ferrule Connector) offers high precision, ideal for dense connections. SC (Subscriber Connector) is easy to connect and disconnect, commonly found in telecom. ST (Straight Tip) connectors are widely used in multimode applications. LC (Lucent Connector) is compact and suitable for high-density installations. D4, DIN, MU, and MT connectors cater to specific networking needs, enhancing performance and versatility in data transmission across different fiber optic systems.</p></p>
