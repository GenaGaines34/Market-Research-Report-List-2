A 3-way valve facilitates multiple flow directions, mixing or diverting fluids. A 4-way valve typically manages actuators by directing air to alternate ports, crucial in double-acting cylinders. Other types may include specialty valves designed for niche applications. These valves are essential in automation and control systems across various industries, enhancing efficiency and precision.</p></p>
