<p><p>Hollow mill market types categorize milling tools based on the number of teeth. The 3-teeth hollow mills are typically used for aggressive cutting in softer materials, offering quicker material removal. The 4-teeth mills provide a balance between speed and finish quality, making them versatile for various applications. Mills with above 4 teeth, such as 5 or 6, excel in producing finer finishes and greater accuracy, ideal for harder materials or precise workpieces, enhancing overall productivity and surface quality.</p></p>
