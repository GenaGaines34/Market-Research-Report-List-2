Evaporative cooling systems utilize water to reduce air temperature, while ambient air systems rely on fan-driven airflow to cool users. Gel/ice pack vests incorporate frozen elements for immediate cooling, and PCM garments use phase change materials to regulate temperature by absorbing and releasing heat. Liquid circulating systems circulate chilled fluids through garments, providing consistent cooling. Each type offers unique features suited for different environments and user preferences.</p></p>
