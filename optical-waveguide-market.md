<p><strong>The Optical Waveguide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Channel Waveguide</li><li>Planar Waveguide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical waveguide market is primarily categorized into channel waveguides and planar waveguides. Channel waveguides are structures that confine light within a narrow channel, allowing for precise control of optical signals, ideal for applications like integrated optics and sensors. In contrast, planar waveguides are flat, layered structures that guide light through a two-dimensional plane, commonly used in telecommunications and display technologies. Both types are essential for advancing optical communication and enabling compact photonic devices.</p></p>
