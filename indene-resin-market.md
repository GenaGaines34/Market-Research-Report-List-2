<p><p>Indene resin is categorized into two main types: ordinary resin and modified resin. Ordinary resin is primarily used for general adhesive applications and provides good thermal stability and clarity. Modified resin, on the other hand, is enhanced through chemical processes to improve its properties, such as flexibility, adhesion, and weather resistance. This makes modified resin ideal for more specialized applications including paints, coatings, and sealants, where enhanced performance characteristics are required. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
