<p><strong>The Surge Protection Devices (SPD) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voltage Switch SPD</li><li>Voltage Limiting Type SPD</li><li>Combination Type SPD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surge Protection Devices (SPDs) are essential for safeguarding electrical systems from voltage spikes. The market features three main types: </p><p>1. **Voltage Switch SPD**: This device disconnects the load when voltage exceeds a certain threshold, preventing damage.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Voltage Limiting SPD**: This type clamps voltage surges to a specific level, allowing safe operation of connected equipment.</p><p>3. **Combination Type SPD**: It combines features of both Voltage Switch and Voltage Limiting types, providing comprehensive protection against various surge conditions.</p></p>
