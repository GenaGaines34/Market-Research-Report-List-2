<p><p>The cash recycling system market comprises two main types: Vertical Cash Recyclers and Embedded Cash Recyclers. Vertical Cash Recyclers are standalone machines that automate the handling, sorting, and storage of cash, enhancing efficiency in retail and banking environments. In contrast, Embedded Cash Recyclers are integrated into existing point-of-sale systems or ATMs, offering streamlined cash management without requiring separate hardware. Both types aim to optimize cash flow, reduce manual handling, and improve transaction accuracy in cash-heavy operations.</p></p>
