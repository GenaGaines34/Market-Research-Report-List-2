<p><p>The boat trailer market primarily features two types: bunk and roller trailers. Bunk trailers utilize long, padded bunks to support the boat, providing stability and ease during loading and unloading. They are ideal for shallow launches and larger boats. In contrast, roller trailers are equipped with rolling supports, allowing for smoother loading and unloading, especially in deeper waters. They offer flexibility and reduced friction but may require more frequent adjustments. Each type caters to different boating needs and preferences.</p></p>
