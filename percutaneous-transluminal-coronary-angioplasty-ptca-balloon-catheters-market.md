<p><p>The Percutaneous Transluminal Coronary Angioplasty (PTCA) balloon catheters market consists of several types, each designed for specific applications. Normal balloon catheters are used to dilate narrowed coronary arteries. Drug-eluting balloon catheters release medication to reduce restenosis. Cutting balloon catheters feature tiny blades to incise plaque, facilitating easier dilation. Scoring balloon catheters have built-in scoring elements to create microfractures in the plaque, enhancing balloon expansion. These innovations improve treatment outcomes and patient safety in coronary interventions.</p></p>
