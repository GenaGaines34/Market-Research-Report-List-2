<p><strong>The Subtalar Joint Implants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cementless</li><li>Cemented</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The subtalar joint implants market is primarily segmented into cementless and cemented types. Cementless implants integrate with the bone through biological fixation, promoting natural healing and reducing the risk of loosening over time. These implants are often preferred for younger patients due to their durability. In contrast, cemented implants utilize polymethyl methacrylate (PMMA) bone cement to bond the implant securely to the bone, providing immediate stability and strength, making them suitable for older patients or those with compromised bone quality.</p></p>
