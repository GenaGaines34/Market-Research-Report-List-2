<p><p>The Exhaust Expansion Joints market comprises two primary types: Flange Type and Welding Type. Flange Type expansion joints feature flanges at each end, facilitating easy installation and disconnection, ensuring efficient alignment with exhaust systems. In contrast, Welding Type joints are permanently affixed through welding, offering a more robust and leak-proof solution for high-temperature applications. Both types are critical in managing thermal expansion and movement in exhaust systems, enhancing durability and reliability across various industrial and automotive sectors.</p></p>
