<p><strong>The IoT RFID Readers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency Readers</li><li>High Frequency Readers</li><li>UHF Readers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT RFID readers market is categorized into three main types: Low Frequency (LF), High Frequency (HF), and Ultra High Frequency (UHF) readers. LF readers operate at 125-134 kHz, ideal for access control and animal tracking due to their short read range and high reliability in challenging environments. HF readers function at 13.56 MHz, commonly used for smart cards and NFC applications, offering moderate read range and data exchange. UHF readers operate between 300 MHz and 960 MHz, providing long-range capabilities, making them suitable for inventory management and supply chain applications. Each type serves distinct needs within the IoT ecosystem.</p></p>
